Our Story

Built at the Intersection of Grit, Growth, and Purpose.

IGNITEDDREAM wasn't born in a boardroom.

It was born in the field — across automotive plants, quality rooms, strategy tables, and silent mountains — built by someone who has sat on both sides of the table: as an engineer who solved real-world problems, and as a seeker who asked deeper questions about human potential.

Founded by Shiv Pandey, an NIT Kurukshetra alumnus and former professional with marquee organizations including Maruti Suzuki, Suzuki Motor Corporation (Japan), MACE, and KPMG India, IGNITEDDREAM is the convergence of two powerful journeys:

🏢

A Corporate Career

Forged through hands-on product development, quality systems, supplier ecosystems, cross-functional execution, and structured business problem-solving.

🧘‍♂️

A Personal Transformation

Catalyzed by extensive solo travel across India, deep introspective study, and intensive Vipassana meditation practice — unlocking a profound understanding of mindset, emotional balance, self-leadership, and the inner drivers behind human behavior.

"That rare combination — corporate execution met with inner mastery — is what IGNITEDDREAM brings to every program, every workshop, every coaching engagement."
