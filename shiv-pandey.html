The Story

The Engineer Who Went Searching — And Found Leadership.

I was never supposed to be a coach. I was an engineer. I graduated from NIT Kurukshetra — one of India's most demanding technical institutions — and walked straight into the world of precision engineering at Maruti Suzuki.

I spent the next years designing transmission cases in Japan, collaborating with the discipline-obsessed engineers at Suzuki Motor Corporation, navigating quality systems, supplier ecosystems, and the brutal demands of high-volume automotive manufacturing.

But a question kept growing louder inside me: Why do some people — equally talented, equally equipped — perform at a completely different level? What is actually driving performance?

The technical systems couldn't answer that. The CAD files couldn't answer that. Even the structured frameworks of KPMG — where I later worked on government initiatives like Startup India — couldn't answer that. So I did something that most people thought was reckless. I left.

I packed a bag, traveled solo across India, sat in silence through intensive Vipassana meditation retreats, and spent almost two years studying the mind, behavior, resilience, and human performance at their roots.

What I found changed everything. The most important variable in any organization isn't strategy, systems, or structure. It's the inner operating system of the people running it — their mindset, their self-awareness, their ability to lead themselves before they lead others.

That discovery became IGNITEDDREAM. I didn't launch a training company. I built a movement — rooted in the belief that real leadership is an inside job. I am not selling motivation. I am offering transformation — and I know the difference.

The Path

From the factory floor to the training room.

2007 – 2009

Asst. Manager — Maruti Suzuki India

Built technical foundations in NX CAD, GD&T, PLM, and localization at one of India's largest manufacturers. The discipline of precision engineering began here.

2009 – 2010

Asst. Manager — Suzuki Motor Corporation, Japan

Lived and worked inside Japan's legendary quality culture — the discipline that now shapes every program I design. A formative, career-defining year.

2010 – 2014

Deputy Manager — Maruti Suzuki India

Led product development, cost optimization, and cross-functional execution at scale. Operating inside demanding, high-volume automotive manufacturing systems.

2014 – 2016

Trainer & Sr. Counselor — MACE / Maruti

Crossed the line from engineer to people developer — delivering leadership, management, and motivation programs across Maruti's vast supplier networks. The calling emerged here.

2016 – 2017

Consultant — KPMG India

Worked on Ease of Doing Business, Startup India, and Stand-Up India. Gained a systems-level view of ecosystems from the top. Completed the corporate chapter.

2017 – 2018

Solo Traveler & Vipassana Practitioner

Left the map. Traveled solo across India. Practiced Vipassana intensively. Read deeply on the mind, behavior, resilience, and decision-making. Rebuilt my understanding of what actually drives human performance — from the inside out. The most important two years of my life.
