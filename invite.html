FAQ

Common Booking Questions.

How far in advance should I book?

+

For large events (100+ participants or conference keynotes), we recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance. For smaller workshops or coaching programs, 2–4 weeks is typically sufficient. We always try to accommodate urgent requests when possible.

Are workshops customized or off-the-shelf?

+

All workshops are fully customized. We begin with a brief call (or questionnaire) to understand your team's context, challenges, and goals — and then design the program accordingly. No two sessions are identical.

Do you travel for in-person events outside Delhi NCR?

+

Yes. Shiv is available for in-person delivery across India and internationally. Travel and accommodation are arranged separately and factored into the engagement fee. Reach out with your location and we'll work it out.

What happens after I submit the form?

+

We'll review your inquiry within 24–48 hours and respond with a brief follow-up call or email to clarify requirements. If it's a good fit, we'll share a customized proposal shortly after.
