FAQ

Common Questions.

Can workshops be customized for our industry or context?

+

Absolutely. All workshops are fully customizable. After an initial brief, we adapt the content, examples, and exercises to your team's specific context, industry, and challenges. No two programs are ever delivered identically.

What is the minimum and maximum group size?

+

Workshops can be designed for intimate groups of 15–20 (for deep work) all the way to large conference keynotes of 500+. The format and delivery style are adapted accordingly.

Do you offer virtual or online delivery?

+

Yes. All workshops can be delivered virtually (via Zoom or Teams), in-person, or in a hybrid format. The engagement quality is maintained regardless of the delivery mode.

How do I get a proposal?

+

Fill out the inquiry form on the Invite Me page with your event details. We'll respond within 24–48 hours with a customized proposal.
