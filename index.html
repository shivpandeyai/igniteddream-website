Our Story

Built at the Intersection of Grit, Growth, and Purpose.

IGNITEDDREAM wasn't born in a boardroom.

It was born in the field — across automotive plants, quality rooms, strategy tables, and silent mountains — built by someone who has sat on both sides of the table: as an engineer who solved real-world problems, and as a seeker who asked deeper questions about human potential.

Founded by Shiv Pandey, NIT Kurukshetra alumnus and former professional with Maruti Suzuki, Suzuki Motor Corporation (Japan), MACE, and KPMG India — this is the convergence of two powerful journeys:

Corporate Execution

Product development, quality systems, supplier ecosystems, cross-functional execution, and structured business problem-solving — lived, not taught.

Inner Mastery

Solo travel across India, deep introspective study, and intensive Vipassana meditation — unlocking mindset, emotional balance, and the inner drivers of human behavior.
